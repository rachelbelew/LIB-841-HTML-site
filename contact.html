Oak Grove Elementary School

Oak Grove Elementary School in Corbin, Kentucky, serves around 705 students from pre-kindergarten through sixth grade, offering a welcoming and close-knit learning environment. The school community is primarily made up of white students, with a small but growing representation of other racial and ethnic backgrounds. With approximately 39 full-time teachers, students benefit from dedicated staff and a strong sense of support. Around 77% of students qualify for free or reduced-price lunch, highlighting the school’s important role in supporting families and ensuring equitable access to learning. Oak Grove consistently performs well academically, with reading and math proficiency rates placing it among the top schools in the state. The school takes pride in its students’ success and continues to foster a positive, achievement-focused culture.
