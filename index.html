About This Website

Hello and welcome! My name is Rachel Belew. I am currently pursuing my Rank I in Library Science. This website is designed to document my journey, interests, and professional growth in the field of educational technology and librarianship.

Throughout this website, you’ll find samples of my work, reflections on what I’m learning, and resources I find valuable for both current and future librarians.

During this program, I have found a passion for integrating technology into library spaces to support student learning and engagement.

My Goals

My short-term goal is to complete my Rank I and begin working in a school library where I can inspire young readers and support teachers. Long-term, I hope to contribute to curriculum development and digital literacy efforts within my district.

Libraries are more than books. They are places where creativity and critical thinking is encouraged and inspired.
